Welcome to the Tsunami Waves Foundation website. This organization was founded by Carlos Martinez in 2015 with the goal of helping children in need. This cause is very close to his heart as he was once one of those children.

carlos realizes there are so many under-priviledged children that without help will never be able to experience certain opportunities and simple pleasures that are so many times taken for granted.
